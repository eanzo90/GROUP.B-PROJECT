About Us

The Founding Story of Visual Tech CompanyIn 2025, Group 2 web Class had a vision to create a platform that would

empower creators to capture life's most precious moments with precision and ease.

With a passion for photography and a keen eye for innovation,

Group 2 web class set out to build a company that would provide high-quality photography and videography

equipment to professionals and enthusiasts alike.

The Spark of Inspiration

Group 2 web Class was inspired by the rapid advancements in camera technology and the growing demand for high-quality visual content.

Recognizing the need for a reliable and trustworthy source of photography and videography equipment,

They decided to take the leap and start VISUAL TECH COMPANY
